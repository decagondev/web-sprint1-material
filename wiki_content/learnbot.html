LearnBot, Your 24/7 Course Companion!

 What is the LearnBot? LearnBot is an AI-powered assistant available around the clock, designed to enhance your learning experience. Wondering where to find it? Look for the LearnBot Bear on the right-hand side of your Canvas course page.

 What can you ask LearnBot? Anything related to your course! For non-academic matters, our Knowledge Base is your go-to.

 Now, what sets LearnBot apart from live chat support? LearnBot is at your service 24/7, providing on-demand assistance. Live chat, on the other hand, is available during specific hours and is operated by our support team.

 So, how does LearnBot work its magic? It's well-versed in your current course progress, modules, and learning preferences. Each chat interaction is tailored to your unique learning style, thanks to AI. It's like having a personal tutor in your pocket!

  If LearnBot ever goes silent, don't worry. Just refresh your browser and try again, ensuring it's not in incognito mode. If that doesn't do the trick, enable third-party cookies in your browser settings. If all else fails, you can always submit a ticket to our Knowledge Base.
